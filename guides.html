Core Process

The 6-Step Packing Method

Follow this process before every trip and you'll never overpack again.

1
Define Your Trip Profile

Note duration, climate, activities, and formality. A 3-day business trip and a 2-week beach holiday need completely different lists.

2
Choose Your Bag First

The bag sets the limit. Pick a 20L–40L backpack or a carry-on roller. Constraint is the mother of smart packing.

3
Build Your Clothing Capsule

Select neutral-colored, mix-and-match pieces. Aim for 1 outfit per 2 days — plan laundry, not extra clothes.

4
Pack Toiletries Last

Use travel-size or solid formats. Buy locally what you can. Toiletries are the #1 source of unnecessary weight.

5
The Remove-3 Rule

After packing, remove 3 items. You probably don't need them. This one rule will change your travel forever.

6
Test & Carry It

Walk around your home with the packed bag for 10 minutes. If it's uncomfortable, re-evaluate. Your future self will thank you.
